# Signing In and Activating Billing

New resources stay locked until your account is activated. Activation comes down to answering two questions: **who do we bill**, and **how do you pay**. Settle both and your billing profile flips to active, opening up every resource type in the portal.

## Create your account

The sign-in screen has a **Register** link — use it to open an account with your email address. That email doubles as your username and is where invoices and notifications will land, so choose the address that belongs on your records. Confirm the email, and you can sign in.

The first time you sign in, Stratos quietly sets up your customer profile and hands you a default project to work in.

## Tell us who to bill

Head to **Billing → Funds**. If your billing profile is still incomplete, this page asks for your billing details before anything else:

- First and last name
- Phone number
- Street address, city, and ZIP/postal code
- Country

Submit the form and the portal acknowledges with "Billing details saved". These values print on every invoice, so keep them aligned with your company or personal records.

## Set up a way to pay

Do either one of these:

- **Deposit funds.** Still on **Billing → Funds**, type an amount and pay by card. It lands in your account balance and gets drawn down by later bills.
- **Save a card.** On **Billing → Cards**, add a debit or credit card. It's verified and kept on file with our payment provider for future charges — no money moves right now.

![Deposit dialog with card element](/docs-img/deposit-funds.png)

Some operators activate accounts a different way — by manually checking your identity or business details instead. If your cloud works like that, the portal tells you what's still pending, and your account activates the moment the operator signs off.

## Confirmation

Once your billing details are saved and either a deposit or a card is on file, your billing profile goes active. From that point the full sidebar is yours: servers, volumes, networks, and everything else.

## Reading your balance

**Billing → Funds** is always your source of truth for where you stand:

- **Balance** — money you've deposited, waiting to cover bills.
- **Account credit** and **Promotional credit** — credits the operator granted you or that you redeemed with a promo code. The same page has a **Promo code** box for redeeming one.

Older invoices and payments are filed under **Billing → Billing history**. For the bigger picture of how those charges are calculated, see [How Metering and Billing Work](/docs/concepts/billing-and-metering).
